Celebrating Our First PhD Graduates

Autonomous Control and Optimization for Robotics · Community

Congratulations to Dr. Ravi Pandya and Dr. Ran (Thomas) Tian—the first two doctoral graduates from the Autonomous Control and Optimization for Robotics (CORTx) lab! Their work on safe generative AI guardrails and human-centered robot learning shaped much of the lab’s DNA. We reflected on their journeys, the collaborations that made them possible, and the new directions they are charting as research scientists.
